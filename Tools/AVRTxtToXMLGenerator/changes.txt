Version 0.11 (2007-02-27)
	* Reads ATmega8, ATmega88, ATmega128, ATmega1287.
	* Declares registers and masks.

Version 0.12 (2007-03-04)
	* Reads slash delimited bit name as two bits.
	* ATmega64 supported.
	* Writes general port declarations.
	* Adds register name in front of one-char bits. (All SREG bits for example).
